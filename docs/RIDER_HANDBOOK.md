## üì¶ Delivery Process

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Receive Order
- Get notification
- Review details (restaurant, distance, pay)
- **Accept** within 30 seconds
- Navigate to restaurant

#### 2. Pick Up from Restaurant
- Arrive at restaurant
- Show order ID on app
- Verify items (check list in app)
- Check special instructions
- Pack carefully in delivery bag
- Mark "Picked Up" in app

#### 3. Delivery to Customer
- Navigate to customer address
- Call if can't find location
- Maintain food temperature
- Be polite and professional
- Hand over order carefully

#### 4. Payment Collection (if COD)
- Collect exact amount
- Verify payment in app
- Give any change
- Thank customer

#### 5. Complete Delivery
- Mark "Delivered" in app
- Take photo if "contactless"
- Get customer signature if required
- Move to next delivery

## üõ°Ô∏è Safety Guidelines

### Road Safety

**Helmet** - ALWAYS wear a proper helmet
**Speed** - Follow traffic rules, don't rush
**Lights** - Use headlights, especially at night
**Maintenance** - Keep bike in good condition
**Weather** - Reduce speed in rain
**Rest** - Take breaks, avoid fatigue

### Personal Safety

**Avoid Dark Areas** - Especially late at night
**Stay Alert** - Be aware of surroundings
**Emergency Button** - Use app's SOS feature if needed
**Valuables** - Don't carry unnecessary valuables
**Cash** - Deposit COD cash regularly

### Food Safety

**Delivery Bag** - Use insulated bag always
**Separation** - Keep hot and cold items separate
**Secure** - Prevent spillage during transit
**Hygiene** - Wash hands, keep bag clean
**Temperature** - Hot food hot, cold food cold
