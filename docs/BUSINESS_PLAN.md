# KhabarLagbe - Business Plan
## Bangladesh Food Delivery Market Entry Strategy

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Company:** KhabarLagbe ("খাবারলাগবে" - "Need Food" in Bangla)
**Industry:** Online Food Delivery
**Target Market:** Bangladesh (180M+ population)
**Initial Focus:** Dhaka Metropolitan Area (22M+ population)

### Mission
To become Bangladesh's most trusted food delivery platform by providing 
fast, affordable, and reliable service while empowering local restaurants.

### Vision
Every Bangladeshi should be able to enjoy their favorite food delivered 
hot and fresh within 30 minutes.

## 2. Market Analysis

### 2.1 Bangladesh Food Delivery Market

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Market Size (2024) | $800M+ |
| Annual Growth Rate | 25-30% |
| Internet Users | 130M+ |
| Smartphone Users | 90M+ |
| Urban Population | 40M+ |

### 2.2 Competitors

| Competitor | Market Share | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------------|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Foodpanda | ~60% | Brand recognition | High commission, slow CS |
| Pathao Food | ~25% | Existing rider network | Limited restaurant selection |
| Shohoz Food | ~10% | Tech platform | Poor UX |
| Others | ~5% | - | - |

### 2.3 Our Competitive Advantages

1. **Lower Commission**: 15-18% vs competitors' 25-30%
2. **Faster Delivery**: AI-optimized routing, dedicated dark kitchens
3. **Local Focus**: Bangla-first UI, local payment integration
4. **Restaurant Partner Program**: Free tablets, marketing support
5. **Rider Welfare**: Better pay, insurance, career growth

## 3. Business Model

### 3.1 Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Restaurant Commission | 15-20% per order | 70% of revenue |
| Delivery Fee | ৳30-80 based on distance | 15% of revenue |
| Advertising | Featured restaurants, banners | 10% of revenue |
| Subscription | KhabarLagbe Pro (free delivery) | 5% of revenue |

### 3.2 Unit Economics (Per Order)

```
Average Order Value (AOV): ৳450
├── Restaurant Commission (18%): ৳81
├── Delivery Fee: ৳45
├── Total Revenue: ৳126
│
├── Rider Payout: ৳50
├── Payment Processing (1.5%): ৳7
├── Customer Support: ৳5
├── Technology & Infra: ৳8
├── Marketing (Amortized): ৳15
├── Total Cost: ৳85
│
└── Gross Profit: ৳41 (32% margin)
```

### 3.3 Pricing Strategy

**Restaurant Commission Tiers:**
- New restaurants (first 3 months): 12%
- Standard: 18%
- Premium placement: 22%
- Cloud kitchen partners: 15%

**Delivery Fee Structure:**
- 0-3 km: ৳30
- 3-5 km: ৳45
- 5-8 km: ৳60
- 8+ km: ৳80
- Rain/late night surge: +৳20

## 4. Go-to-Market Strategy

### 4.1 Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
**Focus Areas:** Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, Uttara

**Targets:**
- 200 restaurant partners
- 500 riders
- 10,000 app downloads
- 5,000 orders/month

**Tactics:**
- ৳100 off first 3 orders
- Free delivery first month
- Restaurant onboarding team (10 people)
- Social media campaign (Facebook, Instagram)
- University ambassador program

### 4.2 Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-9)
**Expand to:** Mirpur, Mohammadpur, Bashundhara, Motijheel

**Targets:**
- 1,000 restaurant partners
- 2,000 riders
- 100,000 app downloads
- 50,000 orders/month

**Tactics:**
- Referral program (৳50 for both)
- Restaurant success stories
- Influencer partnerships
- Corporate tie-ups
- Ramadan/Eid campaigns

### 4.3 Phase 3: Expansion (Months 10-18)
**New Cities:** Chittagong, Sylhet, Khulna, Rajshahi

**Targets:**
- 5,000 restaurant partners
- 10,000 riders
- 500,000 app downloads
- 300,000 orders/month
